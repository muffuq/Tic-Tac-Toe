60 Years of Experience 1957

Aydin family stepped into the textile industry under the name of “Aydin Wool Collective Company” by Ziya Aydin

60 Years of Experience 1985

Started exporting products to Singapore and Saudi Arabia.

60 Years of Experience 1996

Woven upholstery fabric production started.

60 Years of Experience 2000

Production of chenille, polypropylene, and fancy yarn started.

60 Years of Experience 2003

With the new investment upholstery fabric production capacity rose to 20 million linear meters.

60 Years of Experience 2006

Nova was established with an annual production capacity of 60.000 tons in steel wire and 18.000 tons in any kind of foam.

60 Years of Experience 2008

Planet was established with a closed area of 260.000-meter square and furniture manufacturing started.

60 Years of Experience 2013

Knitted mattress ticking production started.

60 Years of Experience 2014

Aydin opened a new upholstery fabric factory in Sakarya with air jet, water jet, and warp- knitted machines.

60 Years of Experience 2017

Aydin moved mattress ticking production to the Kayseri factory.

60 Years of Experience 2019

Aydin moved to the new 360 Showroom in Istanbul

60 Years of Experience 2023

Aydin is the leading upholstery fabric, mattress ticking, and decorative carpet supplier in the world and continues to leave a legacy for future generations.
